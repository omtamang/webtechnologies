He was born on 1987(June 24). He is also known as Leo Messi. He is an Argentine professional football player. His first club was barcelonea. He has scored over 750 goals. He has won ballon d'or for seven times. Until leaving the club in 2021, he had spent his entire professional career with Barcelona, where he won a club-record 35 trophies, including 10 La Liga titles, seven Copa del Rey titles and four UEFA Champions Leagues.
